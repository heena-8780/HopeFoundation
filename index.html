Help students achieve their dreams

14 years old Anisha Bisht loves to dance and study English.

Daughter of a driver father and a housewife mother, Anisha has found wings to her dreams at PYDS Learning Academy.

Not only does she get to learn on a scholarship but she also gets to enjoy her favourite sports like Badminton and Volleyball.

Help abandoned elderly live with dignity

Troubled by old age and living on his own, 80 years old Alok Mishra was struggling to get one square meal a day.

His son and daughter-in-law live in the city and he was forced to fend for himself at this age.

Today, he receives two wholesome meals a day from a community kitchen.

"The food I get is very tasty and comes twice a day, he said."

Studies show over 70% of senior citizens in India face abuse - verbal, physical, emotional and financial. They also live with deteriorating health conditions they cannot afford to treat. This Mission ensures they are not alone, sick and hungry.

Education is a fundamental right for all, but girls from illiterate families are mostly denied this right in India. By the time they reach adolescence, 40% are out of school. This Mission support the education of thousands of girls.

About Us

We are India's most trusted and transparent crowdfunding platform, with a vision to create a social impact.

Our immediate focus is to simplify and facilitate the conception of Human Rights.

We seek to do this by establishing a minimum threshold in which every single human being on the entire planet is provided with what we call The Five Fundamental Human Rights: Food, Water, Shelter, Education and Healthcare.

Vision

  • To help the underprivileged and marginalized to live a better tomorrow through various interventions

Mission

  • Hope Foundation strives to improve the quality of life of the financially disadvantaged and vulnerable sections of the society by promoting meaningful and needs based interventions on education, health and mitigation of poverty.
